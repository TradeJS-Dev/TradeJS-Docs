---
title: 'LiquidityZones'
---

`LiquidityZones` is a built-in TypeScript strategy from `@tradejs/strategies`.

It researches price behavior around detected liquidity zones and can use strategy-specific AI guardrails.

## Use It For Researching

- zone reactions
- quiet derivatives pockets
- liquidity context filters
- runtime/backtest comparison

## Notes

Recent source changes tuned its deterministic AI gate. Validate zone quality visually and compare results across periods before considering automation.
