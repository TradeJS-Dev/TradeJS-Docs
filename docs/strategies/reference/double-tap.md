`DoubleTap` is a built-in TypeScript strategy from `@tradejs/strategies`.

It is designed for double-touch style structures where price retests a zone or level before a decision.

## Use It For Researching

- retest setups
- support/resistance confirmation
- local structure filters
- AI-gated strategy experiments

## Notes

Recent source changes tuned its deterministic AI gate with market context filters and support-touch requirements.

Use backtests, chart figures, and skipped reasons to inspect whether the detected structure matches your intent.
