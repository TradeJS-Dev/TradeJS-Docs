title: 'TrendShift'
---

`TrendShift` is a built-in TypeScript strategy from `@tradejs/strategies`.

It researches trend transition setups and has strategy-specific AI guardrails.

## Use It For Researching

- trend reversal or transition behavior
- breadth shock recovery
- market context filters
- AI gate comparison

## Notes

Recent source work tuned TrendShift around CMC context, breadth shocks, and indicator gates. Revalidate after changing market context, timeframe, or risk settings.
